// about me

Keeping systems fast, secure, and always on.

I'm a System Engineer who loves the space where hardware, operating systems, and networks meet. My day-to-day is about provisioning servers, automating repetitive work, hardening security, and diagnosing the problems others can't reproduce.

I believe good infrastructure is invisible — it just works. So I focus on observability, documentation, and automation so that scaling up never means firefighting. Whether it's a bare-metal box or a Kubernetes cluster, I care about reliability and clean, repeatable processes.

When I'm not in a terminal, I'm learning about cybersecurity, self-hosting new tools in my homelab, and sharing what I break (and fix) along the way.
